# Alarmr

## What
I want a simple alarm clock that tells me the time and wakes me up in the morning. Simple? No.

![image](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019e0e65-299c-7e6d-8d94-7ff058fb07c2/image.png)
### Capabilities
- Ambient light sensor to automatically adjust the brightness of leds
- Large easy to read numbers
- Alarm to wake you up in the morning
     - Custom Audio (song/tone)
     - Adjust Volume
     - Snooze Button

### Requirements
- Easy to use
- Doesn't keep me awake at night
- Wakes me up in the morning
- Readable at any light level

### Constraints 
- Unit price under $40
- Not ugly af
- Smooth buttons
